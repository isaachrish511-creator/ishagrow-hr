Who We Are

Bridging Talent &
Opportunity

Isha Grow HR Consultancy is a professional talent sourcing and placement firm with one clear purpose — connecting the right candidate to the right company.

We are not just a recruitment agency. We are a strategic hiring partner. We go beyond matching resumes to job descriptions — we assess skills, personality, work culture, and career goals to ensure every placement is a long-term success.

We specialize in sourcing freshers and early-career professionals across all major non-IT domains, and we deliver both individual placements and large-scale bulk hiring solutions for businesses across India.

Meet the Founder

A Passion for
Connecting People

Isha Grow HR Consultancy was founded by Christilla, an MBA graduate with dual specialization in Human Resources and Marketing, driven by a deep belief that the right people in the right roles can transform any organization.

With expertise spanning talent management, organizational behavior, labor laws, workforce planning, and employer branding, Christilla brings a unique blend of people expertise and business strategy to every hiring engagement.

Based in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, the consultancy serves clients across industries — helping companies find the teams they need to grow, and helping candidates find careers where they can truly thrive.

Our Promise — Every company deserves a team that drives their vision forward. Every candidate deserves a role where they can learn, grow, and build a meaningful career. At Isha Grow HR Consultancy, we make that connection happen.
